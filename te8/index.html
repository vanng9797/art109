Technical Exercise 8: Paper.js

For this assigment, I explore animation feature of Paper.js. Basing on "Nyan Rainbow" example, I created a field of squared shapes animated across the canvas. What I modified:
- The speed of the shapes
- direction of the shapes to move according to the angle the mouse to the center of canvas
- Animation to change shapes' color
- Adding rotation animation to the shapes
- Adding opacity to the shapes
