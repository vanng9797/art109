City of Abstraction

“City of Abstraction” is a project of creating an imaginary abstract world using 3D model and Three.JS codes.
In this Abstract city, buildings are made of floating cube shapes emerging together into a city.
There are 3 primary cubes that is placed in the center, which represent its city's core power.
They create the gravity of this world, making the city floating on air.
